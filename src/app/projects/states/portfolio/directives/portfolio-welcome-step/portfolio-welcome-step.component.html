Portfolio Preparation

Preparing your portfolio involves 5 steps:

  1. Select your Grade you are applying for
  2. Upload your Learning Summary Report
  3. Select the Tasks you want included
  4. Upload any Other Resources you want to add
  5. Compile your resources into your portfolio and review

Once you have completed all of these steps, your portfolio will be prepared by {{ externalName.value }} and you will be notified when it is ready. You can then check your work, and if you want to make any corrections repeat thesesteps to create a new version of your portfolio.
